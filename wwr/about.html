White Water Rafting

Embark on the ultimate adventure! Experience the thrill of rushing rapids and serene river views. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned rafter, we've got the perfect trip for you.

History

Founded in 1998, Dry Oar Rafting Co. began with a dream to bring the excitement of whitewater rafting to everyone. Our founders, a small team of nature enthusiasts, started with just one raft and a passion for exploring rivers.

Over the years, we've grown into a leading rafting company, offering unforgettable experiences on some of the most breathtaking rivers in the country. From humble beginnings, we now host thousands of adventurers annually while maintaining a commitment to safety, sustainability, and exceptional service.
