Explore Our Trips

Half-Day Trip

Highlights: Quick and exhilarating adventure perfect for beginners and families.

  • Duration: ~4 hours
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Rivers: American River, Lower Kern
  • Ideal for: Families, first-timers, and busy adventurers

Our half-day trips offer a thrilling introduction to whitewater rafting. Experience the rush of Class II and III rapids while soaking in stunning river views. This quick yet unforgettable adventure is perfect for those short on time but looking for big thrills.

1-Day Trip

Highlights: A full day of excitement, challenging rapids, and breathtaking scenery.

  • Duration: ~8 hours
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Rivers: Upper Kern, Middle Fork
  • Ideal for: Adventurers seeking a day of fun and challenges

Spend a full day tackling thrilling Class III and IV rapids. Enjoy breaks for a riverside lunch and moments to take in the breathtaking landscapes. This trip is ideal for those with a moderate fitness level and a thirst for adventure.

Multi-Day Trip

Highlights: Immersive wilderness adventure with camping and gourmet meals.

  • Duration: 2-5 days
  • Difficulty: Challenging
  • Rivers: Rogue River, Colorado River
  • Ideal for: Outdoor enthusiasts and experienced rafters

Embark on the ultimate adventure with our multi-day trips. Navigate Class IV and V rapids during the day and relax under the stars at night. Enjoy gourmet riverside meals, campfire stories, and deep connections with nature. A truly unforgettable experience!
